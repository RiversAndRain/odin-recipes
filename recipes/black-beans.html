Spicy Black Beans

Description

Ultra garlicy, as spicy as you want, and the perfect base for a variety of meals.

Ingredients

  • Dry black beans, soaked (at least a few hours, preferably overnight)
  • Garlic
  • Yellow onion
  • Spices: dry or fresh peppers, red pepper flakes, smoked paprika, salt, cumin
  • Toppings: chopped cabbage, roasted cauliflower, cilantro, salsa, hot sauce, pickled red onion

Steps

  1. Add rinsed beans, water to cover, chopped onion, whole garlic cloves and desired spices to pot.
  2. Bring to a boil, then turn down to low-medium heat, cover and cook til beans are tender
  3. Once beans are tender, cook uncovered to reduce liquid if necessary.
  4. Use fork to lightly mash beans, garlic cloves, and peppers.
  5. Assemble into bowls, burritos, or tacos with desired toppings.
