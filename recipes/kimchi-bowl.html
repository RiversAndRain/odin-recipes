Kimchi Bowl

Description

A simple and delicious bowl that can take advantage of store bought kimchi.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Kimchi
  • Roasted Vegetables (onion, mushrooms, sweet potato)
  • Crispy Egg
  • Sauce: gochujang, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, soy sauce

Steps

  1. Make rice.
  2. At the same time as rice cooks, roast vegetables.
  3. When rice and vegetables are done, fry an egg until crispy.
  4. Assemble bowls with rice, roasted vegetables, crispy egg and kimchi.
  5. Mix sauce ingredients together to taste, and add to bowl.
