Macaroni Seafood Salad

Description

A family recipe that tastes much better than it sounds.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked macaroni
  • 1-2 6 ounce cans tuna packed in water, drained
  • 2 4.5 ouince cans tiny shrimp, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup chopped dill pickle
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp celery seed
  • 1 clove minced garlic

Mix and pour over cooked drained pasta. Let sit for one hour:

  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup pickle juice
  • 1/4 cup salad oil

Mix together then add to pasta:

  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise or vegenaise

Add spices, tuna, shrimp, onion, and pickle to the mix. Better on the second day.
